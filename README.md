# 📖 Project Overview

This project was developed for the **Onyx Data #DataDNA June 2025 Challenge**, where it was recognized as a **Runner-Up**.

The objective was to transform raw social media marketing data into an interactive Power BI solution that enables marketing teams to monitor campaign performance, measure audience engagement, analyze customer behavior, and optimize future content strategies.

The dashboard combines interactive visualizations, dynamic KPIs, and DAX-driven analytics to provide actionable insights across platforms, content categories, post formats, audience interactions, and geographic regions.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

This dashboard helps marketing teams answer critical business questions, including:

- Which social media platforms generate the highest audience reach?
- Which content categories drive the most engagement?
- Which post formats perform best?
- How do customers interact with different types of content?
- Which hashtags improve campaign visibility?
- What are the best posting times to maximize engagement?
- Which regions deliver the strongest marketing performance?
- Which campaigns provide the highest return on marketing efforts?

# 💡 Business Insights

## 📊 Overall Campaign Performance

- Social media campaigns generated **4.27B impressions** and **3.56B views**, demonstrating exceptional brand visibility across multiple platforms.
- Total audience engagement reached **477M interactions**, supported by **623M likes**, **198M shares**, and **143M comments**, reflecting strong audience participation.
- More than **26M clicks** indicate successful traffic generation from social media campaigns to external destinations.

---

## 🎥 Video Content Delivers the Highest Engagement

Video content significantly outperformed every other post format, generating approximately **351M interactions**.

| Content Type | Engagement |
|--------------|-----------:|
| 🎥 Video | **351M** |
| 🖼️ Image | **42M** |
| 📺 Live Stream | **38M** |
| 📝 Text | **22M** |
| 📄 Article | **17M** |
| 🎠 Carousel | **5M** |
| 📑 PDF | **1M** |

**Business Insight**

Video-first content remains the most effective strategy for maximizing audience reach, engagement, and brand awareness.

---

## 📚 Educational Content Builds Strong Audience Engagement

Educational content generated the highest engagement among all content categories.

| Category | Engagement |
|----------|-----------:|
| 📘 Educational | **173M** |
| 📦 Product Promotion | **170M** |
| 🎭 Entertainment | **60M** |
| 👥 Customer Story | **51M** |
| 🎤 Event / Webinar | **23M** |

**Business Insight**

Educational and product-focused content together generated more than **340M interactions**, demonstrating that audiences value informative and solution-oriented content.

---

## 🏷️ High-Performing Hashtags Increase Content Visibility

The dashboard identified several hashtags that consistently generated exceptional engagement.

Top-performing hashtags included:

- **#ProductDemo**
- **#CustomerStory**

Both hashtags exceeded **140M engagements**, highlighting the importance of strategic hashtag selection.

**Business Insight**

Using campaign-specific hashtags can significantly improve discoverability, audience reach, and overall engagement.

---

## 👥 Organic vs Sponsored Content Performance

Content distribution across campaigns showed:

- **80.75% Organic Content**
- **19.25% Sponsored Content**

While organic posts dominated overall volume, sponsored campaigns achieved higher visibility and stronger content amplification.

**Business Insight**

A balanced strategy combining organic community-building with targeted sponsored campaigns can maximize long-term marketing performance.

---

## 🎯 Customer Behaviour Analysis

Click-to-View Ratio by content category:

| Category | CTR |
|----------|----:|
| 🎭 Entertainment | **2.68%** |
| 📘 Educational | **2.52%** |
| 📦 Product Promotion | **2.41%** |
| 🎤 Event / Webinar | **2.19%** |
| 👥 Customer Story | **1.76%** |

**Business Insight**

Entertainment content encourages users to take action more frequently, making it particularly effective for driving website traffic and campaign conversions.

---

## 🌍 Platform Performance

Audience exposure varied significantly across social media platforms.

| Platform | Reach |
|-----------|------:|
| ▶️ YouTube | **1.02B** |
| 🎵 TikTok | **969M** |
| 📷 Instagram | **886M** |
| ✖️ X | **877M** |
| 💼 LinkedIn | **286M** |

**Business Insight**

Video-centric platforms consistently generated the strongest audience reach, reinforcing the growing importance of video marketing in digital campaigns.

---

## 📅 Seasonal Engagement Trends

Marketing engagement fluctuated throughout the year, with noticeable declines during **April** and **September**, followed by a strong recovery in **October**.

**Business Insight**

Campaign performance is influenced by seasonal audience behavior, making campaign timing an important factor in maximizing engagement.

---

## 🌎 Regional Performance

Performance varied across different countries and regions, with significant differences in impressions, engagement, and click-through rates.

**Business Insight**

Localized marketing strategies tailored to regional audience preferences are more likely to outperform a standardized global campaign.

# 💡 Business Recommendations

## 🎥 1. Prioritize Video Marketing

Video content generated the highest engagement, outperforming all other content formats.

**Recommendation**

Increase investment in short-form videos, tutorials, product demonstrations, customer testimonials, and educational reels to maximize audience reach and engagement.

---

## 📚 2. Expand Educational Content

Educational posts consistently generated the highest audience interaction.

**Recommendation**

Develop educational campaigns, industry insights, how-to guides, and expert tips to build credibility, trust, and long-term audience engagement.

---

## 📦 3. Strengthen Product-Focused Storytelling

Product Promotion content ranked among the top-performing categories.

**Recommendation**

Combine product demonstrations with customer success stories and user-generated content to improve awareness, engagement, and conversions.

---

## 🏷️ 4. Standardize High-Performing Hashtags

Hashtags such as **#ProductDemo** and **#CustomerStory** consistently achieved the highest engagement.

**Recommendation**

Adopt successful hashtags as part of future campaigns while continuously testing new campaign-specific hashtags to expand audience reach.

---

## ⏰ 5. Optimize Content Scheduling

Audience engagement varied across different posting times and months.

**Recommendation**

Schedule campaigns during peak engagement periods identified in the dashboard to maximize visibility, interactions, and campaign effectiveness.

---

## 🌍 6. Allocate Marketing Budget Strategically

YouTube, TikTok, and Instagram generated significantly higher audience reach than other platforms.

**Recommendation**

Focus advertising spend and content creation efforts on high-performing platforms while optimizing investment in lower-performing channels.

---

## 🌎 7. Personalize Regional Campaigns

Marketing performance varied across countries and regions.

**Recommendation**

Customize messaging, creatives, and publishing schedules to match regional audience preferences and maximize campaign performance.

---

## 📈 8. Improve Underperforming Content Formats

PDFs, Articles, and Carousel posts generated comparatively lower engagement.

**Recommendation**

Redesign or repurpose low-performing formats into more engaging visual content such as videos, infographics, or interactive posts.
